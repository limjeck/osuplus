# How to obtain osu!api v2 keys

1. Go to your [account settings](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/account/edit).
1. Navigate to the OAuth section and click "New OAuth Application".
   ![New OAuth Application](/assets/api-howto1.png)
1. Enter "osuplus" as the application name. You may leave the application callback URLs blank. Then click "Register application".
   ![Register application](/assets/api-howto2.png)
1. You now have your Client ID and Client Secret! Click "Show client secret" to reveal it (do not show this to anyone).
   ![Keys](/assets/api-howto3.png)
1. In case you changed your client ID or secret, you can modify the keys stored in osuplus via the osuplus settings tab.
